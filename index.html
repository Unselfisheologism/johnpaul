GUIGUI

Vision-Based Tactile Robotic Hand with Real-Time Grasp Planning

Independently designed and built an end-to-end robotic hand for adaptive grasping, combining vision-based tactile sensing and ML-based grasp planning to detect contact and adjust grasps in real time.

Key Results
  • Achieved 90 N gripping force
  • Improved grasp adaptability by ~30% across 6+ object types
  • Delivered 3 prototype iterations in 2 months with hardware cost under €2,000
  • Self designed Interactive GUI with real time feedback

22-DOF Humanoid Hand

Developed a 22-DOF tendon-driven humanoid hand capable of mimicking human hand gestures in real time using a Raspberry Pi 5 and wrist-mounted camera.

Key Results
  • Delivered a standalone humanoid hand system for real-time gesture mimicry
  • Combined embedded vision, gesture tracking, and robotic hand actuation
  • Demonstrated responsive hand-motion replication for interactive demos
Mechanical DesignTendon-Driven MechanismsRaspberry PiArduinoMediaPipeComputer VisionPython3D Printing

11-DOF Humanoid Robotic Hand for 4NE-1

Master's thesis at Neura Robotics: end-to-end design, CAD development, and prototyping of a fully articulated 11-DOF robotic hand for the 4NE-1 humanoid.

Key Results
  • Delivered a functional 11-DOF robotic hand prototype for humanoid applications
  • Achieved compact palm-integrated actuation with 11 linear actuators at 1:1 scale
  • Enabled dexterous grasping and manipulation for the 4NE-1 humanoid platform

Myoelectric Bionic Arm

Bachelor's thesis: design and prototyping of a 3D-printed EMG-controlled prosthetic arm for lower-arm amputees, combining bio-mechatronic design and Arduino-based control.

Key Results
  • Delivered a functional EMG-controlled prosthetic arm prototype
  • Reduced cost by ~70% compared to commercially available prosthetic hands
  • Received the Best Bachelor's Project Award
